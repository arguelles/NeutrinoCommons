Initializes EarthWithCavity object by defining the cavities properties and position.

Parameters:
  • rho_cavity (float) - cavity density [gr/cm^3]
  • ye_cavity (float) - electron fraction [dimensionless]
  • wx (float) - cavity horizontal axis (x-width) [eV^-1]
  • wy (float) - cavity vertical axis (y-width) [eV^-1]
  • th_rot (float) - axis orientations with respect to the equator [rad]
  • deep_cavity (float) - distance of cavity center to Earth's surface [eV^-1]
  • th_cavity (float) - angle measure from the meridian (+y) to the cavity center [rad]
